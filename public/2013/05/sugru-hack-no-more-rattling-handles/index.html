Sugru Hack: No more rattling handles

May 24, 2013

Sounds and vibration travels in this new house that we are in. It was built in the 50’s and has ‘aged’ wood flooring throughout. Whenever I’d walk through the house, I could hear this antique’s handles clanking away. I mean, I don’t stomp around the house by any means! A quick fix with Sugru now dampens the oscillating handles and is now noise-free!
